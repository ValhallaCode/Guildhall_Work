Controls
----------------------------------------
WASD to move
Mouse to look around
F3 to use debug texture
F5 to switch camera mode
F6 to switch between flying and walking
Space to jump and fly up
Control to fly down
Shift to move faster
Mouse wheel to select block and break hookshot
Left mouse to place block and fire hookshot
Right mouse to dig block and reel in hookshot

Bugs
-----------------------------------------
